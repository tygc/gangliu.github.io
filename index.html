Welcome

Gang LIU is an energetic researcher and nice person. His interests lie in the areas of “human learning” (speech signal processing) and machine learning. One of his goals is to advance the robustness and efficiency of speech system. He has successfully lead the CRSS team for the NIST language recognition evaluation (LRE) competition in 2011 and work as a team member for NIST speaker recognition evaluation (SRE) competition 2010 and 2012. He has strong passion for the speech related research and application, in which he believe lies part of his life meaning. He also enjoys community service and sharing with friendship, in which he believe lies another part of life meaning. He is member of the IEEE (ID:92198059), ISCA (ID:12445), and ACM (ID: 7932497)

Research Interests:

  1. Acoustic event identification: Speaker/language/emotion identification
  2. Automatic speech recognition (ASR): computer aided language learning (CALL) for deaf-mute child, ASR in noisy and dynamic condition
  3. Robust feature designing: source separation, speech enhancement, supra-segmental feature
  4. Engineering: efficient algorithm to process big data (>10TB); code delivery to sponsors; High performance computation.
